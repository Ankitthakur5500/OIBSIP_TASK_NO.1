SHREE DURGA AIR COURIER & CARGO

Founded in 1984, Shree Durga Air Courier & Cargo was initially created to bring efficiency to the transportation segment of the Sears supply chain. Serving the wide range of vendors and retail outlets (throughout India) was a great foundation and training ground for delivering just in time services. Over decades later, We still maintains long standing relationships with it's original customers but has added hundreds of other customers, multiple service offerings and a variety of equipment types. All of which has led to our consistent ranking as one of the top 50 for hire carriers in India.
